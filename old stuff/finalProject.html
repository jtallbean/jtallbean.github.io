Road Rage




How to Play


Road Rage is a 2-player game.

The cars drive around in a space filled with walls. The up to 5 bullets each car can shoot cannot go through walls.

However, the center of both cars give them the special ability to drive through objects, including walls and other cars.

But a bullet hitting this almighty center of the car can destroy the car.

The controls for the first player are:

Arrow Keys to move. Space to shoot.

The controls for the second player are:

ASDW to move. Q to shoot.
